Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

StackTracker is a Bitcoin portfolio tracking app built for privacy. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

StackTracker does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account or login credentials
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Financial or transaction data

All portfolio data, transaction history, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. Nothing leaves your phone.

Third-Party Services

StackTracker connects to the following external services to provide functionality:

  • CoinGecko API - Used to fetch current and historical Bitcoin price data. No user-identifiable information is sent. CoinGecko Privacy Policy
  • Mempool.space API - Used to look up public Bitcoin address balances and transaction history when you choose to track a cold storage address. Only the Bitcoin address you provide is sent. Mempool.space About

These services receive only the minimum data required to fulfill your request (price lookups or public address queries). No personal data, device info, or analytics are transmitted.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. StackTracker does not use cloud storage, remote databases, or any server-side infrastructure. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

No Accounts

StackTracker does not require or support user accounts, sign-ups, or login of any kind.

No Advertising

StackTracker contains no advertisements and does not integrate any advertising SDKs or tracking frameworks.

Children's Privacy

StackTracker does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
